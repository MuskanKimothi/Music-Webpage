"The best, most beautiful, and most perfect way that we have of expressing a sweet concord of mind to each other is by music."

-Jonathan Edwards

Music is a part of every known society, past and present, and is common to all human cultures across the globe. The emotions caused by music, the attitudes of its composers and players, and the venues it is played can also vary from time to time and place to place.
